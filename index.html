About Me

Hi, I'm Josiah. I fell in love with wedding videography when my best friend asked to film a wedding with him. Seeing how much those moments meant to them inspired me to pursue this beautiful craft professionally.

Although I'm just starting my journey with two weddings under my belt, I bring fresh eyes, contagious enthusiasm, and a commitment to creating films that genuinely reflect each couple's unique love story. My approach is unobtrusive yet attentive, ensuring I capture all the authentic emotions and details of your special day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from couples

I recommend booking as soon as you have your wedding date and venue secured to ensure availability. Popular dates (especially weekends in summer and fall) often book 2-3 months in advance. That said, I sometimes have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to inquire even if your wedding is coming up soon.

My current turnaround time is 6-8 weeks for the highlight film and 8-10 weeks for any additional edits (ceremony, speeches, dances). I pride myself on delivering beautiful films in less time than many competitors while still maintaining my high standards for quality. You'll receive a digital preview first and have the opportunity to request minor tweaks if needed.

Absolutely! I love destination weddings. Travel within 50 KM is included in my packages. Beyond that, I charge reasonable travel fees depending on location. I base in Krakow Poland, I require the couple to cover transportation. I'll handle all equipment logistics and provide you with beautiful footage no matter where you say "I do."

To secure your date, I require a 30% non-refundable deposit with a signed contract. The second payment of 30% is due 90 days before your wedding, and the final 40% balance is due 14 days prior to your wedding date. I accept all major credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and checks. For destination weddings, travel costs may be required upfront when booking flights.
